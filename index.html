Features

Everything You Need to Grow

Built with intention — lean, fast, and respectful of your time and data.

The Garden

Your financial overview in one calm view. Upcoming renewals, monthly totals, category breakdowns — all visible at a glance without the noise.

Gmail Auto-Detect

Connect Gmail to automatically scan for subscription emails and plant them in your garden instantly. Finds Netflix, Spotify, Adobe and 50+ more.

Renewal Alerts

Never get surprised by a charge again. Urgent flags highlight anything renewing within 3 days so you can uproot it before it hits.

Smart Categories

Auto-sorted beds — Entertainment, Software, Health, Gaming, and more. Filter to see exactly which bed your money is growing in.

Private by Default

Everything lives in your browser — nothing leaves your device. No account, no tracking, no servers holding your financial life.

Bank CSV Import

Upload a statement from any bank and Sprout automatically spots recurring charges — no manual entry, no guessing.
